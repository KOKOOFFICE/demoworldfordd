    • Design

    Brochure is a booklet that contains bulk of valuable information. The purpose of the brochure is to sell the product and/ or services to its customer. The work to design an productive brochure starts way before the actual design starts


    Before going into the designing process, we start out with the proper groundwork, knowing your brand identity, your ideal customers that will make the whole process to go seamlessly for us and for yourself.


    The brochure is identified differently depending upon the industries. We design various kind of brochure such as menu cards, travel itinerary, fashion catalogue, product pamphlet et cetera.

    Knowing your Brand Identity

    Brand identity is the package of the logo, color, tone, the font of the brand that makes it unique and distinctive. All the above constitute the brand Identity, the vital component. So our designers first make sure that your brochure aligns with your brand identity.

    Ideal Customer

    We at High Think Media box knows that the information should be designed and presented according to the requirements of the ideal customer. We design the layout of the brochure in such a way depending up on the requirements of the target audience.

    Building your Message

    The message plays a very vital role in the brochure design. So we design the brochure in such a way that the message in communicated in a way that the audience can relate to and understand effectively. Brochure is used to immense amount of information of the products.

    At High Think Media Box the brochure design go through the following steps:

    • Tri-fold brochure
    • Z-fold brochure
    • Parallel fold brochure
    • Roll fold brochure
    • Accordion fold brochure
    • Single gate fold
    • Double gate fold
    • Half + half fold
    • Half + tri-fold
    • Half-fold
    • Half-fold (letter)

    Creating a draft and collecting the required Images

    Before starting the proper design our designers starts by creating a rough draft or blue print of the brochure to get an idea of the layout, font, size and colors that can be used in brochure design.

    Structured Text

    Our designers structure the text to make it readable and given interesting headings and sub headings to grab the attention of the user, so that the users can skim through the content.

    Out-of-the box thinking

    Users look for something unique. The template kind of brochure can easily put off the curiosity of the user, so our designers come up with distinctive brochure designs.

    The CTA is the focal point

    The call to action (CTA) should be placed in first place of the brochures. We place the CTA at the vantage point of the brochure instead of burying it somewhere in the block of text.

    High Think Media box is the brochure maker who is well known for providing quality brochure for our clients.
